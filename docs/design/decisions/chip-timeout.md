# Chip Selection Timeout

**Decision**: Timer expires = you get nothing. No consolation, no random pick.

## Rationale

Maximum pressure is the identity (Pillar 5: Pressure, Not Panic). The timer punishes *indecision*, not lack of knowledge. A prepared player who knows the chip pool has enough time to read, compare, and choose.

Over time, players internalize the chip pool and decisions become faster, more instinctive. The pressure is psychological: you *feel* rushed even when you have enough time, because the timer is always there.

Softening the timeout (random pick, lesser reward) would reduce the stakes of the selection screen. The chip select should feel like a decision that matters — and skipping it should sting.
