# Chip Synergies

**Decision**: At least 30% of chips must interact with other chips' effects. Chips are a synergy web, not a stat list.

## The Problem with Independent Stat Mods

If every chip is an independent buff (pierce more, move faster, hit harder), runs produce *different* builds but not *surprising* ones. The player optimizes a stat priority and the build is predictable from the first few offerings. This is variety, not emergence.

## The Goal: Emergent Interactions

The best roguelite builds create effects the designer didn't explicitly program. Isaac's Brimstone + Tammy's Head. Slay the Spire's infinite loops. Balatro's multiplicative joker chains. These emerge from system interactions, not individual item power.

## Interaction Types

| Type | Example | Why It Works |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Scaling** | "Bonus damage per unique chip kind held" | Rewards diversification, creates tension with stacking |
| **Trigger amplifier** | "Piercing counts as an impact for overclock triggers" | Makes amps feed overclocks — cross-kind synergy |
| **Conditional** | "While below 2 lives, bump grade is always perfect" | Creates dramatic moments, risk/reward |
| **Build-around** | "Double all augment effects, but amps deal 50% damage" | Forces a specific build direction, high commitment |
| **Threshold** | "At 5+ total stacks across all chips, gain +1 bolt" | Rewards investment regardless of build path |

## Design Rules

1. **No chip should be an island.** Every chip interacts meaningfully with at least 2-3 others.
2. **Synergies should be discoverable, not obvious.** The best interactions require experimentation or community knowledge.
3. **Some synergies should be stronger than the sum of their parts.** Two mediocre chips that combine into something powerful = a discovery worth sharing.
4. **Trade-off chips create the hardest decisions.** "Benefit + cost" chips are more interesting than pure buffs because they make the player evaluate their build as a system.

## Pillar Alignment

- **Pillar 2 (Build the Broken Build)**: Synergies are what make builds "click." Without them, builds are collections of buffs.
- **Pillar 7 (Discovery)**: Undocumented synergies are the discovery layer. Wiki content.
- **Pillar 9 (Every Run Tells a Story)**: Synergistic interactions create unique moments. "I didn't know those two chips combined like that" is a story worth telling.
