# Phase 9: Boss Nodes & Advanced Mechanics

**Goal**: The endgame content that tests mastery and creates the most memorable run moments.

## Boss Design Philosophy

Bosses are the highest-variance moments in a run — they're where builds are tested, where clutch plays happen, and where the most shareable stories come from. Every boss encounter should feel like a unique situation, not a stat check.

## Alpha Nodes

Mini-boss encounters that test specific skills:

- Denser layouts with tougher active mechanics
- Each Alpha has a **gimmick** that interacts with the chip system: one might be weak to piercing, another punishes wide breakers, a third rewards shockwave builds
- Gimmick interaction means the same Alpha plays differently depending on the player's build — the build determines the strategy, not just the execution

## Omega Nodes

Multi-phase boss encounters:

- **Phase transitions**: Boss changes behavior at HP thresholds. New cell patterns, new hazards, new mechanics per phase.
- **Build-reactive difficulty**: Boss behavior adapts to the player's chip loadout. Piercing build? Boss spawns shielded cells. Shockwave build? Boss spaces cells far apart. This prevents any single build from trivializing every boss.
- **Guaranteed unique moments**: Each Omega boss has at least one mechanic that creates a "did you see that" moment (arena-wide attack to dodge, last-stand desperation phase, etc.)

## Advanced Bolt Mechanics

New bolt behaviors unlocked in late-game:

- **Curve shots**: Breaker tilt influences bolt trajectory mid-flight (not just on bump). High-skill mechanic.
- **Charge shots**: Hold bolt on breaker to charge, release for a powerful single hit. Risk/reward — holding the bolt costs timer time.
- **Split shots**: Bolt splits on impact with specific cell types. Creates visual chaos and multi-target damage.

These mechanics interact with existing chips — a piercing curve shot, a charged shockwave hit — multiplying the late-game situation space.

## Difficulty Modes

- **Selectable starting tier**: Skip early tiers for experienced players (separate from run modifiers — this is a convenience feature, not a challenge modifier)
- **Reward multipliers**: Higher starting tier = less Flux (you skipped nodes)
- **Endless mode**: No win condition — tiers keep escalating until you die. Leaderboard by highest tier reached. The ultimate situation generator — how far can your build carry you?
